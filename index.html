my go-to latte recipe

March 4, 2021

In this post I want to share the perfect latte recipe I have come up with with after lots of trial and error!

Start with making a double shot of your favorite espresso. Mine is the Lavazza Espresso Barista Perfetto Whole Bean Coffee 100% Arabica.

Next, I take about 1/4 cup of oat milk and steam and froth it until there is about half an inch of foam.

Before mixing the milk and espresso, I take the Vanilla Bean Paste from Trader Joe's and mix a tiny amount into the espresso. I find this adds just the right amount of sweetness without overpowering the espresso!

Finally, combine the milk and espresso and enjoy your homemade vanilla bean oatmilk latte!
